What is Aviator Gambling?

Aviator is an online casino game developed by Spribe and launched in 2019. The game involves a 2D plane taking off, with gamblers placing bets on when the aircraft will ‘fly away’. The longer the plane stays in the air and doesn’t fly away, the larger the bet multiplier.

This unique approach means gamblers can place wagers before the plane takes off and cash out before it ‘flies away’. There’s no telling when the aircraft will fly away ‚Äì so if a gambler doesn’t cash out in time, they’ll lose the bet.

The Aviator game also incorporates social features, as gamblers can see who else is playing at the same time. The pane on the left of the screen highlights who has cashed out in each round and who still has their bet running ‚Äì with some platforms also showing a leaderboard of who has multiplied their stake the most.

How to Play Aviator Online with Crypto

Many of the best Tether casino sites will offer Aviator, including Lucky Block Casino. However, those who have never played Aviator before may find it very different from ‘traditional’ casino games. With that in mind, let’s take a closer look at how the game works:

  • Before the plane takes off, players can place two separate bets of the same (or different) stakes
  • Once the plane takes off, the multiplier will gradually increase ‚Äì and players can opt to cash out their wager at any time
  • The longer the plane goes, the higher the winnings will be ‚Äì so players must decide whether to risk waiting a little longer
  • If the plane ‘takes off’ before the player has cashed out, the player will lose the stake.
  • Aviator is a randomized game, meaning there’s no way of forecasting the point at which the plane will ‘take off’ ‚Äì each new run is unique¬†

Most Aviator platforms incorporate an ‚Äòauto-bet‚Äô feature that automatically places the same bet for each round. Some even include an ‚Äòauto cash out‚Äô button, which allows players to specify a multiplier amount where their stake will be cashed out in each round.

Aviator Gambling Strategy

Choosing an Aviator gambling strategy can be challenging since this game is entirely randomized. However, some approaches can help minimize risk, such as:¬†

  • Setting the auto cash out button to 1.2x for each round. By doing this, the risk is minimized ‚Äì yet returns are also significantly decreased
  • Placing one large bet and cashing out almost immediately whilst also making a small bet and letting it run. This has the added benefit of ensuring the gambler doesn’t ‘leave money on the table’.

Ultimately, no strategy can increase a player’s odds since there’s no way of predicting the point at which the plane will take off. However, many players will skip rounds until there have been a few low multipliers in a row ‚Äì in the hope that the next run will feature a high multiplier.
