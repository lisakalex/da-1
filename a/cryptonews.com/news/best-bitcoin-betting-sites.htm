How to Pick the Best Bitcoin Betting Site

Secure Crypto Payments

When funding a Bitcoin betting account, players can easily securely deposit funds – the process is much the same as funding a crypto exchange account. 

It’s simply a case of transferring Bitcoin to the sportsbook wallet address and then waiting for the provider to credit the funds.  Crucially, this means that there is no requirement to share any sensitive financial information with the best Bitcoin betting sites. 

This is in stark contrast to how traditional sportsbooks work, which require players to manually enter their debit/credit card details to make a deposit. 

Fast Deposits & Withdrawals

When it comes to payment speeds, the best Bitcoin betting sites offer lightning-fast deposits and withdrawals. 

Regarding the former, leading providers in this space will credit the betting account after just one blockchain confirmation – which typically takes 10 minutes.  Withdrawals are even more impressive, as the best Bitcoin betting sites will instantly approve a cash-out request. 

This means that there is no requirement to wait 1-3 days for the payments team to authorize the withdrawal, as is often the case with traditional betting sites and sportsbooks that deal with fiat money. 

Crypto Bonuses and Promos

Another huge benefit of opting for a crypto betting site is that oftentimes, platforms in this space offer lucrative Bitcoin casino bonuses.  We found that the best Bitcoin betting sites offer attractive bonuses for both new and existing players.

Low Crypto Banking Fees

The best Bitcoin betting sites enable players to deposit and withdraw funds on a fee-free basis.  This means that while the provider will not impose any transaction fees, the only charge to consider is that of the Bitcoin blockchain.  

In many cases, mining fees will sit below the $1 level – irrespective of how much is being deposited or withdrawn into the Bitcoin betting site. 

Fair Deposit & Withdrawal Limits

When researching the best Bitcoin betting sites, we found that top providers offer fair deposit and withdrawal limits. On the one hand, this means that players can often get started by meeting a minimum deposit of just $5-10. 

When it comes to withdrawals, we found that the best Bitcoin sportsbooks do not impose any cash-out limits.
