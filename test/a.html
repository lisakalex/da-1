Introducing Algolia NeuralSearch

We couldnt be more excited to announce the availability of our breakthrough product, Algolia NeuralSearch. The world has stepped into a new reality of AI chatbots, self-driving cars, drug discovery, and now search. Algolia is the only end-to-end AI search and discovery platform. As some readers will know, AI search is usually powered by vectors and large language models (LLMs). As good as those technologies are, theyre simply not able to scale well enough for the kinds of real-world production workloads our customers have, and certainly not at an economically viable price that makes sense. Vectors and LLMs are large and typically require specialized databases and hardware. To surmount these challenges, we have developed a new technology known as neural hashing. We use neural networks to hash, or compress, vectors to a fraction of their normal size while still retaining up to 99% of the information, and we do it without specialized hardware. Consequently, the performance and price of Algolia NeuralSearch is second to none in the industry. Algolia NeuralSearch combines both vector and keyword search into one product, and because of the speed/cost equation were able to apply both keyword and vector search to every query, delivering superior results.

Bernadette Nixon

Chief Executive Officer and Board Member at Algolia
